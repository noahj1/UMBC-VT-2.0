UMBC Virtual Tour 2.0

The most realistic 3D tour of UMBC within reach of a modern web browser.

umbcvirtualtour.com
Welcome to a new and improved virtual tour for the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. Developed using Unity and built with WebGL, the UMBC Virtual Tour 2.0 allows you to explore the campus in 3D simply by accessing the website from a modern web browser. The tour guides you through instructions to explore the 3D environment in a very quick and intuitive manner. Unfortunately, mobile platforms are currently not supported.
